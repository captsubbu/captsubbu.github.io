Capt Subbu

Captain Leads – Expand The Known & Explore The Unknown

Warrior Entrepreneur Coach, Author, Motivational Speaker, Facilitative Trainer, Adventurous Explorer, Birder, Naturalist, Versatile Multifaceted Personality & a Recipient of Army Bravery Medal

Author of well-acclaimed books on self-Engagement titled ‘Romancing de Life: Live Smart & Live a Royal Life’ published in 2012 & ‘Warrior Entrepreneur’ published in 2020, Capt Subramoniam sails the ‘Camp Catapult’ that steers the Skill Enhancement / Leadership Programmes in addition to Adventure Activities and camping for various segments of the society.

An active Motivational Speaker, Capt Subbu also carries out the unique ‘Workplace Warrior’ Programmes imbibing ‘Art of Warrior Entrepreneur Leadership’ for the Working Professionals as well as Business Owners in addition to Out Bound Training (OBT), Teambuilding Programmes, Soft Skills, Managerial Skills & Special Skills Training, Nature Trails, Treks & Camps with Jungle Survival Activities, Rock Climbing, Jummaring, Rappelling, Counseling on Stress Management & Time Management, Guiding & Mentoring aspirants candidates for SSB Interview to get into Defence Services as Commissioned Officers, Basic Foundation Course for Competitive Exams, etc.

Recipient of a Bravery Medal from the Indian Army , an Appreciation Medal from the US Navy, Young Multifaceted Achiever Award from the local society, Capt Subbu got appreciations from corporate world and educational institutions for his contribution on positive approach towards life.
