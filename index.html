Books Authored by Capt KR Subramoniam

‘Romancing de Life – Live Smart - Live a Royal Life’

(Published in 2012)


This book is dedicated to everyone who aspires to grow up and succeed in life. I believe that every human being can live the life of a Prince or Princess – the Royal Life. The three main constraints to live a Royal Life are Money, Time and the Background or Opportunities. My experience with various experiments prove that these constraints are not obstacles in the path of living a Royal Life. I am a living example that one can lead a royal life by acquiring various skills. This book is prepared with a purpose in mind. It is neither a book of definitions nor a training manual; it is neither a field guide nor a biography. It is just the manifestation of my personal experiences with various experiments, which I had undergone intentionally or unintentionally, that really motivated me to further explore in multi-directions. In this book, one can go through the various experiences that I have undergone. Most of them are explained briefly using suitable examples wherever possible to embellish the concept. Since I get satisfaction and pride in my own growth, I thought I should share my experiences with others so that it helps them to explore in their own way, identify themselves and actualize their potential and live a Royal Life.

‘Warrior Entrepreneur – GROWING and GLOWING’

(Published in 2020)


This book is neither a research paper nor a detailed philosophical guide on ‘glowing or enlightenment’ in life. This just deals the practical ways of living and achieving the ultimate objectives in life by applying simple and practical efforts that neither require special training nor user’s manual for applications to yield powerful effects. Maximum efforts are put to express the concepts in a compact and compressed form enabling the readers to derive their own perceptions to suit their own expectations on growing and glowing. Swami Vivekananda says, ‘Man wishes to fly like a bird, sing like a cuckoo, dance lie a peacock and swim like a fish. But man doesn’t wish to live like a man.’ A man born on this mother earth grows automatically as is the case with all other living organisms. In the animal kingdom man looks different due to his ability to stand upright. Animals live a routing life with their five senses. The gift of the sixth sense makes man different from other animals not only in appearance but also in attitude. The thinking ability combined with extraordinary communication skills make man not only grow but also glow in life. Growth should be an overall growth. Like how all the physical organs of a child grow proportionately when the child grows, the personality growth should also be an overall simultaneous growth; else, the person will appear or behave handicapped in different forms at different stages of life. These nature-gifted positive advantages also have the in-build negative disadvantages and if not nurtured properly with care, will make a person’s life glooming instead of glowing. You ‘Deserve’ what you ‘Desire’ with ‘Passion’

‘Karma to Dharma – Ultimate before Ultimatum’

(Published in 2023)


As we journey through life, we often find ourselves questioning our purpose and direction. We may feel lost, confused, and uncertain about where we are headed. In these moments, we benefit from guidance and tools to help us identify our true purpose and pave a path towards fulfilling it. That is precisely what this book sets out to do. It provides a roadmap to help you discover your life’s purpose and create a future that aligns with your passions and goals. By aiming, achieving, and acknowledging your objectives, you create a life that is fulfilling, satisfying, and joyful. The author reminds us that every person is born with a specific purpose, but it is up to us to identify it and pursue it. The book offers practical tips and strategies for understanding and accepting our past while creating a future that is in line with our deepest desires. By exploring our inner calling and taking action towards our objectives, we achieve our life’s purpose with confidence, ease, and comfort. The author’s deliberate decision to number the pages in reverse order is a gentle reminder that time is fleeing, and each moment is precious. As we read through the pages of this book, we are reminded to live intentionally, with purpose and direction. In a world that can often feel chaotic and overwhelming, this book serves as a guidepost for those seeking clarity and direction. It offers actionable steps towards identifying and achieving our life’s purpose and reminds us that we have the power to create the life we desire.
